Stone Blind

Stone Blind by Natalie Haynes

Publication date: September 2021

In "Stone Blind," readers are taken on a thrilling journey through ancient Greece as they
follow the investigation into the murder of Helene, a young woman found dead in the temple
of Artemis. Alexandra, a blind lawyer, is hired to defend one of the suspects and quickly
becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of politics and deceit.

Through intricate plotting and vivid descriptions of the ancient world, Natalie Haynes creates
a captivating mystery novel that explores themes of power, politics, and ancient
gods. With its rich and detailed setting, "Stone Blind" offers a fascinating glimpse into a
bygone era.

Published in 2021, "Stone Blind" is a compelling addition to the genre of historical fiction that
will appeal to anyone who enjoys a good mystery with a strong sense of place and time.
