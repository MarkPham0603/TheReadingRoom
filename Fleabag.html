Fleabag: The Scriptures

Fleabag: The Scriptures by Phoebe Waller-Bridge

Publication date: May 13rd, 2021

"Fleabag: The Scriptures" is a collection of the complete scripts from the popular BBC
television series "Fleabag," along with additional new material from creator and star Phoebe
Waller-Bridge. This collection offers fans an exclusive and inside look at the making of the
series and a deeper insight into its characters and themes.

The show follows the life of Fleabag, a witty and self-destructive young woman navigating the
modern world in London. The character delivers candid and humorous monologues
to the audience, breaking the fourth wall and inviting viewers into her world as she
struggles with love, loss, and sex.

Waller-Bridge's writing is sharp, candid, and honest, featuring complex and
flawed characters that feel both real and relatable. Along with the complete scripts from both
seasons of the show, this collection includes Waller-Bridge's notes and introductions on the
creation and development of the series.
